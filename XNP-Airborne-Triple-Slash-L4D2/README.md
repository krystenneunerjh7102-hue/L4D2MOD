# XNP Airborne Triple Slash / XNP 空中三段斩

Game: Left 4 Dead 2  
Version: v0.7.7  
Type: Pure VScript gameplay addon  
Steam Workshop: https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3751981994

## English

XNP Airborne Triple Slash is a pure VScript melee gameplay addon for Left 4 Dead 2. It does not require SourceMod or VSLib.

Basic controls:
- Equip a melee weapon.
- Hold Left Shift to prepare the skill state.
- Press Mouse Left for Attack Dash.
- Release Left Shift for Normal Dash.
- When controlled by a Smoker tongue, press Left Shift once to actively escape.

Main features:
- Ground Dash
- Airborne Triple Slash
- Attack Dash / Normal Dash
- Three-charge system
- Active Smoker Tongue Escape
- Tank movement and attack suppression
- Tank stagger attempt
- HUD charge display
- Fall protection

Known limitations:
- Tank physical displacement may be limited by the Source engine and map collision.
- Enable only one XNP melee gameplay addon at a time to avoid script conflicts.

已知限制：
